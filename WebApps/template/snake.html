General Information

The snake is a legless reptile belonging to the suborder Serpentes. Snakes are carnivorous, with flexible jaws allowing them to swallow prey much larger than their heads. They inhabit diverse environments worldwide.

Interesting Facts

  • Some snakes can go months without food after a large meal.
  • Snakes smell with their tongues by collecting scent particles.
  • There are over 3,000 species of snakes found around the world.
